Sculpture Garden Tour

The Z&O Sculpture Garden displays our collection public sculptures from local artists. Open for summer only, join us in a guided tour and explore the unique artworks hidden around the Z&O grounds. Tour runs rain or shine, umbrellas provided, please wear suitable footwear for walking.
