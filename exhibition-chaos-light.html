Danish artist Håkon Wium Lie's transforms the Z&O Art Museum's south galleries creating five thematic 'rooms', featuring his large-scale light installations. The artist explores light as sculpture objects, as emotive sensory elements, examining how light, shadow and color can change our perspective of space, movement, and our mood.
