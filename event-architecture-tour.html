Z&O Architecture Tour

The Z&O Art Museum building has a rich history in the Central Business District. Architect Bruno Stroustrup designed what was originally an office block in the mid 1950s. In 2016 it was converted to the Z&O Art Museum while keeping many of its original brutalist architectural features. This guided tour will take you to through the iconic building and learn about its history.
